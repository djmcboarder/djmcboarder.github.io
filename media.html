Music and Memory

It's interesting how music can capture a moment like a photograph and burn it into the mind. When hearing it again you're transported back.

When my father was ill I was driving from Manhattan to the hospital frequently. One sunny day in October after visiting him when he wasn't doing well, I was driving south, sun in eyes, tears rolling down my face with this song on the radio. Every time I hear it I'm back in that moment.

Arcade Fire, Ready To Start
