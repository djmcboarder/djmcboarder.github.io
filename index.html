Robert G. McNamara

It Was All About Sports

The Yankees made the playoffs this year and elated, I thought of my Dad, the person responsible for my interest in sports. He would have been pleased to see the Bronx Bombers (aka Baby Bombers) with their young players and determination doing well this year. A sports fan with more knowledge than I could ever hope to attain, he was always interested in talking about anything he had a passion for and it was contagious. So it was with mixed emotions that I watched the playoffs, October 17th was the seventh anniversary of his passing.

Robert in Florida
