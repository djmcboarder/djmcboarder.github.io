A Tribute to My Dad

My Dad had a big influence on me. His enthusiasm for life, music and sports were attributes that he passed on. Due to his love of baseball (he was a Rays fan later in life) it was difficult for me to watch the playoffs during the anniversary of his passing... I was thinking about him a great deal. So, when it came time to choose a project, creating a tribute site made sense. Working on it was much harder than I anticipated though and it caused a shadow of sadness around me. Several times I decided to stop. However, challenges are what make us stronger and we can all use more strength so I kept at it.
