A Tribute to My Dad

My Dad had a big influence on me. His enthusiam for life, music and sports is something he passed on. Due to his love of baseball (he was actually a Rays fan later in life) it was difficult to watch to the playoffs during the anniversary of his passing... I was thinking about him a great deal. So, when it came time to choose a project creating a tribute site made sense. Working on it, however, was much more than I bargined for as it's resulted in a shadow of sadness and I've stopped several times. However, challenges are what life's about so kept at it.

Thinking about his life along with his many positive attributes there were areas which would have benefited from his attention and focus. Although he wasn't the best father I loved him and accepted him for who he was. He was a wonderful, imperfect man.

Kelli and Mac
